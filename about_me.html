About Rachel and Her Yoga Journey

I first discovered yoga while working as a solicitor and it quickly became my go-to for unwinding after a busy day, helping me clear my mind and reconnect with my body. Over time, I realised yoga was giving me so much more than just a good stretch. It was creating space to slow down, breathe, and feel more balanced in a fast-paced world.

I've been practising for over ten years now, and I fell in love with its ability to calm the mind and nourish the body. Wanting to share that with others, I trained as a yoga teacher with the British Wheel of Yoga.
